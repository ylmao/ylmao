Curriculum Vitae

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science, Nanjing University, working with Prof. Sheng Zhong. Now, I am a visiting scholar in College of William & Mary, working with Prof. Qun Li. Before that, I got my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2013. My research interests include but not limited to, computer networks, security and privacy. Recently, I am working on privacy-preserving deep learning and wireless network security.
