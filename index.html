Curriculum Vitae

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science, Nanjing University, under the supervision of Prof. Sheng Zhong. In 2017, I was a visiting scholar in College of William & Mary, working with Prof. Qun Li. Before that, I got my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2013. My research interests include but not limited to, computer networks, machine learning, security and privacy.
